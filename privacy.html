🧘 MONK WATCH – Privacy Policy

MONK WATCH (“the App”) respects your privacy. This App is designed to function without collecting or storing any personal data.

📦 Data Collection

We collect no personal information: no names, no emails, no contacts, no location data.

📊 Permissions & Their Use

  • Usage Stats Access: Used to check which app is active and adjust the clock overlay accordingly. Data stays on-device.
  • Overlay Permission: Required to display the clock on top of other apps.

This app uses no analytics tools and no third-party SDKs. It works fully offline.
